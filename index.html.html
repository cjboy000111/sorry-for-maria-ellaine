March 31

My dearest Maria Ellaine,

I am writing this message with a heavy heart, but also with honesty. There are so many things I want to say to you, and I hope you can feel that every word here comes from a real place inside me.

First, I want to say that I am truly sorry. Not just a simple sorry, but a deep and sincere one. I am sorry for the times I hurt you, for the times I made you feel pain, and for the mistakes I made that caused distance between us.

You were never just someone in my life. You became someone very special to me. You became my comfort, my happiness, and the person I looked forward to talking to every single day.

I miss the simple moments we shared. The laughs, the conversations, the small things that made ordinary days feel better. Those memories are still in my heart.

I want you to know how much I loved you. Not in a perfect way, because I am not a perfect person, but in a real way — with true feelings, with care, and with sincerity. My love for you was never fake.

I am trying to grow now. I am learning from what happened. I am thinking about my actions, and the kind of person I want to become. I want to be better.

Whether or not things go back to the way they were, I will always be thankful that you became part of my life. You will always have a special place in my heart.

Will you forgive me?

Because this heart still cares about you.

❤️
